Loei

Loei, Thailand—a hidden gem in Isan—charms with its cool climate, lush mountains, and rich culture. Highlights include:
Phu Kradueng National Park: Stunning waterfalls, pine forests, and epic sunrises from the cliff’s edge.
Phu Ruea National Park: Cool weather, flower fields, and the iconic “Sea of Mist” at dawn.
Local Culture: Unique traditions like the Phi Ta Khon ghost festival and vibrant silk weaving villages.
Food: Try spicy Isan dishes like som tam (papaya salad) and sai krok Isan (fermented sausage).
Perfect for nature lovers and those seeking an off-the-beaten-path adventure!
